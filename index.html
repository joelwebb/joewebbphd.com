Vitality Robotics

At Vitality, we partner with a variety of scientific companies automating their workflows using cloud-native technologies.

Our work involves developing and testing new machine learning applications, optimizing performance and security, and ensuring scientists can Digitze their lab, or automate workflows from data analysis to drug discovery on AWS.

As CEO, our team has designed 16 different SaaS applications for international scientific companies. From designing GxP systems on AWS to building the worlds first fully automated benchtop surgical devices for rodents, we staff B2B contracts with principal AI developers to automate your custom workflows.
